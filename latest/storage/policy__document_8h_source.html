118 /**
119  * Define a policy document.
120  *
121  * Policy documents allow HTML forms to restrict uploads based on certain
122  * conditions. If the policy document is expired or the conditions are not
123  * satisified, POST'ing the form will not succeed.
124  *
125  * @see https://cloud.google.com/storage/docs/xml-api/post-object#policydocument
126  * for general information on policy documents in Google Cloud Storage.
127  */
